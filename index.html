Our Methodology

Why Frameworks
Protect You

When a breach happens — or an auditor arrives — "we did our best" is not a legal defense. NIST and PCI-DSS give every security decision a documented, repeatable, industry-recognized basis that regulators, insurers, and boards respect.

NIST
NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)

The gold standard for building and measuring a security program. Used by government agencies, healthcare systems, and enterprise organizations worldwide. We use it to assess where you are today and map a clear, prioritized path forward.

  • Identify your assets, risks, and current control gaps
  • Protect with controls matched to your actual risk tolerance
  • Detect threats through continuous monitoring strategies
  • Respond with a plan your team can execute under pressure
  • Recover faster with documented procedures and improvement cycles
PCI
PCI Data Security Standard (DSS)

If your business accepts, processes, or stores payment card data — PCI-DSS compliance is mandatory. Non-compliance means fines, failed audits, and the potential loss of your card processing privileges. We get you ready before the auditors arrive.

  • Define and reduce your cardholder data environment scope
  • Network segmentation and firewall architecture review
  • Access control and authentication (Requirements 7–8)
  • Logging, monitoring, and alerting program design
  • Evidence collection and documentation for QSA audits
